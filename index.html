Who is A.J.?

A.J. Dalbany Garrett is an inspiring AI/ML Data Anaylst. She was born and raised in the Acadiana area of Louisiana. Her adolescent dreams were to become a writer and Art Curator while exploring the world full-time. With the launch and popularity of Myspace, AJ became engrossed in computers and technology; thus, deferring her art & writing career. She utilitized her clerical skills and ability to learn programs quickly to obtain administrative and operations roles in Tech. She has over 15 years in the Technology sector, and over 21 years in the corporate environment supporting senior management and C-Suite.


Currently, AJ is a student in the cohort of the Tech Transformation Academy - Web Development instructed by Digital Crafts and at City of Refuge.
